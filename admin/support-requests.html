FAQ Management

How do I track my order? ▼

Go to your dashboard and click on "My Orders" to track your order status. You can also track from the tracking page using your order ID.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept bKash, Nagad, Card payment, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

How long does delivery take? ▼

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available in Dhaka within 1-2 days.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer 7-day easy returns on most products. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
